Pronunciation Shadowing Practice

Listen, repeat, record, and compare your pronunciation with the original phrase.
Watch the video, listen to the phrase, record your own version, then compare the visual acoustic patterns.
What is shadowing? Shadowing means listening to a model and repeating it as closely as possible, paying attention not only to individual sounds, but also to timing, rhythm, stress, and intonation.
